Welcome to Udaan Foundation

Udaan Foundation is committed to uplifting lives and unlocking the potential of underserved communities across India. We work at the grassroots level to promote education, women empowerment, healthcare, and skill development.
Our mission is to give wings—Udaan—to every dream that deserves to soar. Whether it’s a child needing school supplies or a mother seeking vocational training, we’re here to help them rise.
Join us in creating a more just, compassionate, and empowered society—one step, one life at a time.

About Us

Founded in 2010, Udaan Foundation empowers underprivileged communities with education, healthcare, and livelihood programs. We believe in sustainable change through active participation.

Our volunteers and donors are the backbone of our mission. Together, we reach thousands every year with love, hope, and support.
